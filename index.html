One Life Home Healthcare

One Life Health Care offers premium home health care services for various kinds of patients who may require continued attentive health care services ranging from post-hospitalisation to end-of-life health care - all in the comfort of their own homes. Their key objective when they approached us, was to increase the number of online leads, for which their website needed a major upgrade. We were entrusted with building out the flow, sitemap, content, layout, and design direction of their new 30+ page website. The website was to include dynamic functionalities, like an appointment booking system, payment portal, and the most interesting & challenging one - a body map that would help visitors navigate to various healthcare services offered by One Life. With the view of getting the best possible results, we reached out to 5 different website vendors, evaluated each of them on 5 different key strengths we were looking for, and presented the client with the best alternatives. Over the next several weeks, we worked on creating the sitemap, rewriting & creating content for the 30+ page website, as well as directing the layout, flow & design of the website. The result? A beautiful cutting edge website with all of the dynamism and ease of navigation, simple & understandable information that will not overwhelm a visitor, and one very happy client for Inception!
